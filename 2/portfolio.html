A Flinging Game

Personal, 16/6/2021

I wanted to make a small game just for my friends and me, and also to test my skills in this 2-day challenge! Yes, I made that game in 2 days. Basically, you need to fling other players with your fling tool, you can unlock new tools by getting more flings! There's a shop where you can view all of the fling tools, there are also 2 leaderboards - the first is for donations and the second is for flings. You can play the game here.
